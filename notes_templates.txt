==============
TEMPLATES (& CSS)
==============
layout is a template for all templates.
calls in all sorts of external fonts, bootstrap, highcharts etc.
look to static/css
also calls main_new.css - our modifications to those imported themes.

master_panel sets out a template grid used for data entry / forms.
master_panel is populated/modified by panel header information and table data.
the tdata.htmls supplies the rows to tailor one master_panel;
panel header info doesn't need a template: text supplied via views.py / patex
